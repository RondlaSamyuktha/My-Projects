Director: Mani Ratnam

3. BASHA:

Starring: Rajinikanth, Nagma, Raghuvaran.
Director: Suresh Krissna

5. PREMA PAVURALU:

Starring: Salman Khan, Bhagyashree.
Director: Sooraj Barjatya
Plot: "Prema Pavuralu" follows the romantic journey of two individuals from different backgrounds who overcome societal barriers to find love and happiness together.

2. PREMALAYAM:

Starring: Salman Khan, Madhuri Dixit.
Director: Sooraj Barjatya

4. GUNA:

Starring: Kamal Haasan, Rekha, Roshini.
Director: Santhana Bharathi
Plot: "Guna" portrays the unique love story of a mentally affected man, who kidnaps a wealthy girl, mistaking her for a fictitious character named Abirami, set in a dilapidated church in Kodaikanal.
Source: Youtube

6. BOMBAY:

Starring: Arvind Swamy, Manisha Koirala.
Director: Mani Ratnam
Plot: "Bombay" depicts the forbidden love between Hindu student Shekhar and Muslim schoolgirl Shaila, navigating religious tensions and familial opposition.
