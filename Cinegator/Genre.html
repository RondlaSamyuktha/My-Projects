GENRE


DRAMA :

These are serious stories that hinge on events that regularly happen in everyday life. These film and TV series portray realistic characters in conflict with either themselves or with others. They usually focus on character and how these people arc over time. They usually take place in a home, office setting, or with a group of characters forced to interact day to day.

ACTION/MASS :

Action movies are defined by their lead characters and villains. Usually, they're very clear-cut, with no grey area between right and wrong. Action films and series usually thrust our heroes into challenges that typically include fighting, gun or swordplay, chase scenes, and extraordinary feats. The hero usually wins and the villain usually loses.

HORROR AND SUSPENSE :

Horror is a genre of storytelling intended to scare, shock, and thrill audience. Horror can be interpreted in many different ways, generally there is a central villain, monster, or threat that is a reflection of the fears being experienced by society. Suspense is all about building tension and keeping the reader or viewer on the edge of their seat, wondering what will happen next.

COMEDY :

The comedy genre covers any work in film or television whose general purpose is to create humor and intentional laughs for the audience. Comedies usually feature pratfalls, wordplay, uncomfortable situations, and sometimes lean the opposite way of realism. Their stakes are usually personal and violence is almost always done for laughs and not serious consequences.

ROMANCE :

The romance genre is a type of storytelling that focuses on love and romantic relationships between characters. It explores themes of passion, intimacy, and emotional connection and takes many forms in literature, film, and television, including historical romance, contemporary romance, paranormal romance, and romantic comedy.

THRILLER AND CRIME :

Thrillers are dramas that are full of physical conflict. They're not as concerned about the emotions of people in the story as they are about putting those characters in danger. They pack in unexpected twists and amp up tensions. Most crime drama focuses on crime investigation and does not feature the courtroom.
